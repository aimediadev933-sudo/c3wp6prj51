About Our Study

This project conducted a detailed feasibility study for expanding the Trans-Eurasia Information Network (TEIN) connectivity through the Nepal Research and Education Network (NREN) across all seven provinces of Nepal.

The study assessed institutional readiness, ICT infrastructure, policy alignment, and financial viability, laying the foundation for a strategic and sustainable rollout of NREN connectivity to bolster digital inclusion, support research collaboration, and improve higher education access nationwide.

Our Mission

To assess the feasibility and propose a strategic roadmap for the expansion of TEIN/NREN connectivity to higher education institutions across Nepal, creating a comprehensive network infrastructure for the digital future.

Key Activities

Institution Mapping

Mapped 1,432 higher education institutions across Nepal, filtering to 137 feasible candidates for NREN connectivity expansion.

Organized 13 sensitization sessions with 132 high-level officials from 68 institutions to raise NREN awareness.

Evaluated network reach and capabilities with major ISPs, identifying 8 suitable providers for connectivity.
